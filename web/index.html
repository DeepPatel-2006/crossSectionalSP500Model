How it works.
  1. Universe. Top 500 by market cap, $5M+ daily dollar volume, rebuilt every month as of that date, so no survivorship bias.
  2. Features. 49 signals per stock: multi-horizon volatility and momentum, path shape, liquidity, and SEC-filed fundamentals (profitability, value, issuance), all lagged to what was knowable at the time.
  3. Model. Five LightGBM rankers (different seeds) trained walk-forward (each year predicted using only earlier years), then rank-averaged.
  4. Book. Long the top 30, short the bottom 30, inverse-volatility weighted, then weights are projected orthogonal to market beta and the top-5 statistical risk factors.
  5. Discipline. 5 bps/side costs on every trade, and 2024-26 held out untouched until the single final evaluation.
Research model, not investment advice. All results are simulated net returns at the stated cost assumption; the S&P line is the equal-weight average of the same universe, not the index itself. The occasional universe artifact (e.g. a leveraged-ETF proxy) can appear in the book. Past and simulated performance do not guarantee future results.
